About F1Dash.net

Introduction

Welcome to F1Dash.net, your ultimate source for live updates, timings, and in-depth analysis of Formula 1 races. We are dedicated to providing fans with the most comprehensive and up-to-date information about F1 races, drivers, and teams.

Technology and Development

We utilize the innovative tool developed by tdjsnelling on GitHub, known as Monaco. While leveraging this tool, we've made numerous enhancements to tailor it specifically for F1Dash.net. We continuously work on adding new features and improving the user experience, ensuring that our platform remains at the forefront of F1 race data analysis.

F1Dash.net is supported by a robust infrastructure, including over 5 failover servers. This ensures that our platform remains operational and accessible during critical race weekends, providing reliable and uninterrupted service to our users.
